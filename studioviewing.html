Viewing Studio Sessions

We run MusicWRLD out of our studio in Los Angeles, CA at 3515 Oakwood Circle. So we not only provide you with the service of MusicWRLD, but we have our studio which is available to either view certain artists recording tracks, or you may also book a studio time for yourself. If the latter interests you clicking on "Recording Studio Booking" at the top of the page will guide you to where you would fill out the information regarding bookings.

Different artists come here every day, but on some special occasions every month we have some artists who are willing to have people come and view their studio sessions. Of course there is a limit on the number of people who are permitted to watch as we do not want the artists to be overwhelmed. So it is first come, first serve.
